Red-Eyed Crocodile Skink

RECS's Home

Exo Terra Natural Terrarium Large/Tall(90*45*60). Volume 240 liters.

Decoration

You can find interesting ideas on Mike Tytula or Reptiliatus channels for example or any other.

Temperature

Day time: the cool end (an inch above the substrate) 24-26℃; the warmer end (at the level of the basking site) 27-28℃.

At Nigth the the temperatures are maintained between 21-22℃.

If the temperature is in drops below 20℃ it is recommended to utilize a ceramic heat emitter (does not give off visible light).

Humidity

The humidity in the skink’s enclosure should be kept at 70-90%.

Lighting

The UVB(5.0 UVB) light should be on 12 hours a day during the day light portion of the light cycle.
